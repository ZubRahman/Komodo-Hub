Protecting Indonesia's Wildlife

At Komdo Hub we want the public to become apart of our family to help save our endangered animals here in Indonesia. Below are some things you can do to help us.

Learn

Learn more about our mission as to why here at Komodo Hub our main aim is to save our endangered animals in Indonesia. Learn about the factors causing the extinction of our animals and how you can help prevent this.

Participate

Participate in the efforts to save our animals by joining our communities here at Komdo Hub. By joining us you are able to help with saving our animals by creating new habitats, lowering pollution and also separating endangered species from other species in the habitats.

Report

Share any sightings of our endangered wildlife with us at Komodo Hub and we will do our best to recover them and bring them to safety. Also report any issues you find such as habitat issues and highly polluted areas.
