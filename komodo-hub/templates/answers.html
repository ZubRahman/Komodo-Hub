Komodo Quiz - Correct Answers

  1. One of the main reasons for habitat loss is:

    B) Deforestation

    Deforestation is one of the primary causes of habitat loss worldwide, particularly in regions with high biodiversity like Indonesia.

  2. Which of these following species is native to Indonesia?

    C) Komodo Dragon

    The Komodo dragon is endemic to Indonesia, found on several islands including Komodo, Rinca, Flores, and Gili Motang.

  3. What is one of our main goals here at Komodo Hub?

    B) Identifying and protecting endangered species

    Komodo Hub is dedicated to conservation education and efforts to identify and protect endangered species and their habitats.

  4. What is one of the main threats to Indonesia's endemic species?

    B) Habitat loss and deforestation

    Habitat loss due to deforestation, agricultural expansion, and development is a major threat to Indonesia's unique biodiversity.

  5. How can local communities contribute to conservation efforts?

    B) Participating in habitat restoration

    Local community involvement in habitat restoration projects is crucial for successful long-term conservation outcomes.

  6. What is one way the public can get involved in Komodo Hub's mission?

    A) Volunteering for conservation projects

    Volunteering for conservation projects is an effective way for the public to directly contribute to Komodo Hub's mission.
