About Komodo Hub

Komodo Hub is a comprehensive platform dedicated to conservation education and citizen science. We connect schools, communities, and individuals with the resources they need to learn about and contribute to conservation efforts.

Our mission is to foster a deeper understanding of biodiversity and inspire action to protect endangered species and their habitats.
