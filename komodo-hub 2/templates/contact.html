Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our support line.

Can I schedule a demo of the platform?

Yes! We offer personalized demos for schools, communities, and organizations. Please select "Partnership Opportunity" in the contact form and mention you'd like a demo.

Do you offer technical support for the platform?

Yes, we provide technical support to all our users. You can reach our support team via email at support@komodohub.org or through the in-platform help center.

Can I visit your office in person?

Yes, visitors are welcome by appointment. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to schedule your visit.
