Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How can I get started with Komodo Hub?

Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for an account on our platform, choose a plan that fits your needs, and you'll have immediate access to our resources and tools.

Do you offer special pricing for educational institutions?

Yes, we offer special discounted rates for schools, universities, and educational organizations. Please contact our sales team for more information about our educational pricing options.

How can I contribute to conservation projects through Komodo Hub?

There are many ways to contribute! You can participate in our citizen science initiatives, volunteer for conservation projects, or support our partner organizations. Once you're logged in, visit the "Get Involved" section to explore opportunities.

Can I use Komodo Hub resources for my classroom?

Our platform is designed with educators in mind. We offer curriculum-aligned resources, lesson plans, and interactive activities that can be easily integrated into your classroom teaching.
