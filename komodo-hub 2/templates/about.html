About Komodo Hub

Connecting communities to conservation through education and citizen science

Our Story

Komodo Hub was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate conservationists, educators, and technologists who shared a vision of making conservation education accessible to everyone.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a platform that connects communities with the resources they need to learn about and contribute to conservation efforts, particularly focusing on the endangered Komodo dragon and its ecosystem.

Today, Komodo Hub has grown into a comprehensive platform serving schools, communities, and individuals around the world. We provide educational resources, citizen science opportunities, and a space for conservation-minded individuals to connect and collaborate.

Our Mission

To foster a deeper understanding of biodiversity and inspire action to protect endangered species and their habitats through accessible education and citizen science.

Our Vision

A world where communities are empowered with knowledge and tools to actively participate in conservation efforts and create a sustainable future for all species.

Our Values

  • Education for all
  • Scientific integrity
  • Community engagement
  • Conservation action
  • Collaboration
