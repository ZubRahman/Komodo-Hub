Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Komodo Hub's Privacy Policy. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

We take your privacy seriously, especially when it comes to children's data. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, and profile information.
  • School or Organization Information: For institutional accounts, we collect information about your school or organization.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our platform, including learning progress, conservation activities, and contributions.
  • Location Data: When you report wildlife sightings, we collect location information to map these sightings accurately.
  • Communications: When you contact us, we keep records of your communications.

2.2 Children's Privacy

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our platform
  • To process and manage your account
  • To facilitate conservation research and education
  • To communicate with you about updates, features, and news
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our platform
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: We will share your information when you have given us permission to do so.
  • For Research Purposes: We may share anonymized wildlife sighting data with conservation researchers and organizations.
  • Service Providers: We work with third-party service providers who help us operate our platform.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights or the safety of others.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access and receive a copy of your personal information
  • The right to correct or update your personal information
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to restrict or object to our processing of your personal information
  • The right to data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided at the end of this policy.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, and maintained on, computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

If you are located outside the United Kingdom and choose to provide information to us, please note that we transfer the information to the United Kingdom and process it there.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By email: privacy@komodohub.org
  • By mail: Komodo Hub, 123 Conservation Way, London, EC1A 1BB, United Kingdom

Have Questions About Our Privacy Practices?

We're committed to transparency and protecting your data. If you have any concerns or questions, please don't hesitate to reach out.
