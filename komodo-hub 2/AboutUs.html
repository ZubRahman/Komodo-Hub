About Us

Our Mission

Komodo Hub is a digital platform dedicated to the conservation of Indonesia’s endangered endemic species. Through collaboration with local communities, the platform aims to protect and restore biodiversity by offering educational resources, species identification tools, and opportunities for people to get involved in conservation programs.

The Problem

Indonesia's rich biodiversity is facing an alarming decline. The Sumatran Tiger, Javan Rhinoceros, Bali Mynas, and other species are rapidly disappearing due to habitat destruction, pollution, illegal trade, and diseases. Despite years of government funding for conservation, these species are still on the brink of extinction. There is a critical need for a national platform that integrates efforts to protect these species, educate the public, and provide tools for involvement in conservation work.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a digital hub that empowers individuals, communities, and organisations to participate in wildlife conservation in Indonesia. Komodo Hub will offer access to a wealth of information on endangered species, local conservation initiatives, and practical ways to get involved. Our platform will help people of all ages and backgrounds to learn about and act on the threats facing Indonesia’s biodiversity.

Our Values

Community Empowerment

We believe that local communities are the key to successful conservation. Komodo Hub will give communities the tools to take ownership of conservation efforts and be directly involved in protecting their environment.

Collaboration

We work together with government agencies, non-profits, and other organisations to maximize the impact of conservation efforts. Partnerships are essential to achieving long-term sustainability.

Innovation

We embrace technology and innovation as powerful tools for environmental protection. Komodo Hub uses cutting-edge digital solutions to educate, track species, and engage citizens in conservation efforts.

Our Aims

Conservation Education

We aim to raise awareness about endangered species in Indonesia and the importance of biodiversity. Komodo Hub will offer educational resources, including articles, videos, and interactive tools, for users to engage with.

Species Protection

Komodo Hub will help track and protect Indonesia's endangered species by providing data on their current status, habitats, and conservation needs. We aim to contribute to species recovery efforts through active monitoring.

Community Involvement

Our platform aims to engage local communities in conservation through volunteering, citizen science projects, and educational outreach programs that encourage positive environmental actions.
