Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get involved with conservation efforts?

There are many ways to get involved! You can volunteer with our organization, participate in our educational programs, donate to support our conservation efforts, or spread awareness about endangered species in your community.

Do you offer educational programs for schools?

Yes, we offer a variety of educational programs for schools, including virtual classroom sessions, field trips, and curriculum resources. Contact our education team for more information.

How can I report a wildlife sighting?

You can report wildlife sightings through our website by visiting the "Report Sighting" page. Your reports help us track and monitor endangered species populations.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, as a registered charity, all donations to Komodo Hub are tax-deductible. You will receive a receipt for your donation that you can use for tax purposes.
