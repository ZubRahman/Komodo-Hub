Preserving Wildlife for Future Generations

Join along with us to protect our endangered species by using community involvement, and educational efforts to ensure our animals are saved from harmful ecosystems.

Discover how you can join many others by contributing to a worldwide effort to build a sustainable future while still taking in the beauty of nature. Together, we can make a huge difference in preserving not just our animals but our amazing planet and every contribution small or big matters.

Our Conservation Mission

Explore Conservation is dedicated to protecting wildlife and natural habitats around the world. Through collaboration with local communities, educational programs, and conservation initiatives, we strive to create a sustainable future for all living beings.

Challenges Facing Endemic Species

There is a huge threat to Indonesia's endemic species such as the Sumatran Tiger, Javan Rhinoceros, Bali Myna, Javan Eagle, Tarsius, and Celebes Crested Macaque. The main threats are habitat loss and pollution. With your help, we can stop this from happening.

Community Involvement

Here at Komdo Hub one of our main aims is to involve our local communities as much as possible to help protect our endangered species. However, we are not just limited to local communities as we have remote communities too. If you are not local to Indonesia you can still become a part of our community and continue to help save our animals.

Educational Programs

We like to involve primary schools in our efforts to save our animals. At Komodo Hub, we believe the younger generations and generations yet to come should be well educated on the topic and it's amazing to see our children getting involved here at Komdo Hub. We do not want to limit our efforts, so any age is welcome.
