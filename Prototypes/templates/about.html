About KOMODO HUB

A digital platform dedicated to community-supported animal conservation in Indonesia.

Our Mission

KOMODO HUB aims to create a national conservation tool by 2024 that connects schools, conservation communities, and the general public to protect Indonesia's endangered species.

Our Vision

We envision a future where Indonesia's rich biodiversity is preserved through education, community engagement, and collaborative conservation efforts.

What We Do

  • Provide a comprehensive knowledge base about endangered species
  • Develop structured conservation education modules for schools
  • Enable community contributions through research and sighting reports
  • Create a secure platform for schools to integrate conservation into education
  • Connect conservation communities with educational institutions
  • Facilitate government and foundation support for conservation initiatives

Our Team

KOMODO HUB is led by a dedicated team of conservationists, educators, and technology experts committed to protecting Indonesia's wildlife through innovative digital solutions.
