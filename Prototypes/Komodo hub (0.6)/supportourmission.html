Protecting Indonesia's Wildlife

At Komodo Hub, we want the public to become part of our family to help save our endangered animals here in Indonesia. Below are some things you can do to help us.

Learn

Learn more about our mission and why at Komodo Hub our main aim is to save endangered animals in Indonesia. Explore the factors causing their extinction and how you can help prevent this.

Participate

Participate in the efforts to save our animals by joining our communities here at Komodo Hub. By joining us you are able to help with saving our animals by creating new habitats, lowering pollution and also separating endangered species from other species in the habitats.

Report

Share any sightings of our endangered wildlife with us at Komodo Hub and we will do our best to recover them and bring them to safety. Also report any issues you find such as habitat issues and highly polluted areas.

Donate

You can help support our mission by donating to create better habitats, help with rescue operations, and move habitats to places away from prey.

Volunteer

You can volunteer within programs to protect and rehabilitate our endangered species.

Share

Share information about our endangered species on social media. Make sure to tag us!.
